# Introducing english.now

english.now is built around a simple idea: learning English should feel useful from the first session.

Too many language tools stop at flashcards, isolated grammar drills, or scripted exercises that never sound like real life. english.now is designed for learners who want to practice the kind of English they actually need in conversations, work, study, and everyday situations.

## What you can expect

- AI conversations that help you practice speaking in realistic scenarios
- feedback on grammar, fluency, and clarity
- pronunciation support to help you sound more natural
- vocabulary review that helps new words stay with you
- lessons and exercises that can adapt as you improve

## Why this matters

Real progress happens when practice is consistent, relevant, and connected to how people actually use language. That is the experience english.now is aiming to create.

Instead of treating English like a list of rules to memorize, the goal is to make practice feel active, personal, and immediately applicable.
